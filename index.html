Who I Am

I create beautiful, easy to use websites. When I start a project, I have to dig into the details. The customer, the product, the goal. When I can't do that, I find I'm pretty useless. I work with smart teams that "get it" and see the benefit of going through all of the steps. I get most excited when I'm given the chance to turn complicated problems into simple products.

I always install beta software the moment it's available. My wardrobe is primarily made up of t-shirts and tennis shoes. The background picture shown is my actual office. I was a suit wearing commercial banker in a past life. I have three kids, a beautiful wife and a mountain biking addiction.

I am often available to work on interesting projects with a smart team. Want to talk about a new project or anything else? Feel free to shoot me an email at wes@iamweswilson.com.

Who I've Worked With

OpenStack hired me to work closely with their marketing team to design elements of a campaign to show their benefits to enterprise customers. I am currently working with OpenStack to redesign their website and supporting assets.

VentureLab introduces kids to entrepreneurship through experiential camps and curricullum. During my time as Director of Product, I designed and launched the company's first website. As the team grew, my focus was on strategy, creative direction, and scaling technology.

Originally named ZippyKid, Pressable is one of the original managed WordPress hosts. I worked with their small team to create both a marketing site and customer control panel. In addition to web properties, I focused on strategy and marketing assets.

I started Brandstack, a marketplace for original logo and domain name packages, in 2008. Our team never grew beyond 5, so I bounced between design, dev, and management. Brandstack grew to 30,000+ users and was acquired in 2011. It exists today as Brandcrowd.
